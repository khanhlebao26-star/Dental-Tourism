Dental Tourism

We are building a dental tourism platform to help international patients easily access high-quality dental services in Vietnam at reasonable costs. In the current (demo) phase, the platform focuses on providing transparent information about clinics, supporting initial consultations, and suggesting treatment plans tailored to each user's needs. Through the combination of dental consultations and travel planning, we aim to help users understand costs and treatment procedures, and confidently make decisions before booking services.
